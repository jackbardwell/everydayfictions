🔮 Everyday Fictions is the collaborative practice of Jack Bardwell, Richard Fussey and Emilie Loiseleur. Combining our experience in designing spaces, and installations for cultural institutions and communities, we use spatial design to speculate on future realities and initiate public discourse.
